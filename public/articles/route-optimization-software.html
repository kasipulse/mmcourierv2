What Is Route Optimization Software?

Route optimization software calculates the best and most efficient path for a driver to follow when delivering parcels. It considers factors like distance, traffic, delivery priority, package type, and geographic areas.

The goal is simple: deliver more parcels in less time, using less fuel.
