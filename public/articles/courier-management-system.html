What Is a Courier Management System?

A courier management system (CMS) is a software platform that helps logistics companies manage daily operations — from parcel tracking and trip sheet management to billing, route optimization, and POD uploads. The goal is simple: streamline your workflow, reduce human error, and deliver parcels faster.

Introducing MM Courier

MM Courier is a South African-built courier management system designed for delivery companies that handle parcels from partners like FedEx, Seabourne, Brima, and more. It automates key processes such as:

  • Scan-in and scan-out of parcels using mobile devices or Bluetooth scanners
  • Real-time parcel tracking for clients
  • Driver trip sheet generation and route optimization
  • Proof of Delivery (POD) uploads with signature and image support
  • Client-specific billing and invoicing control
